Industry Specialisation
We believe that the best way to master a job is to do it repeatedly and work on getting better at it over time. Our understanding of the commercial cleaning industry is developed by listening to our clients’ feedback and continuously improving our services to better meet their needs. We classify jobs into 2 categories; Commercial and Residential. These categories enable us to better meet the specific requirements of our clients meaning we can focus on what we do best.
Happy Cleaners

Our cleaning team is the most valuable asset we have. All our cleaners work with us on long-term basis. Providing excellent work conditions for them has always been a priority for us, which typically results in a quality service and client satisfaction. We carefully screen and train our cleaners and guarantee that they create the results we promise to provide.
Quality Assurance

Our Quality Assurance System is continuously reviewed to improve our service. This system covers our supervision, inspection, and scoring processes. We rely on these processes to give you our assurance that the provided service meets the quality, and health and safety standards we have in place. The system guarantees routine quality control site inspections to ensure your peace of mind.
Detail-Clean Rotation

Avoid ever needing a spring-clean again! We divide your premises into zones and detail-clean a zone each visit. This thorough cleaning ensures your premises gets the level of clean it needs at the right frequency. This system is part of our vision to provide a complete single solution at the best quality.
